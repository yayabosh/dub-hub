But First!

Allow us to run in Incognito mode, so we can still provide insight to you!

Extensions → Manage Extensions → Dub Hub → Details → Allow in Incognito (ON)

Privacy

All data is stored on your computer's disk, but will still sync with any devices running Chrome that are logged into your account. At any time, you may erase your data by going to this extension's options page and clicking 'Clear Data'. This will remove all local and synchronized information stored by the extension until you browse to another website.
